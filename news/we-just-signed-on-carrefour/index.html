Make it in the Emirates campaign launch

Alsayegh partnered with the Ministry of Industry and Advanced Technology to deliver the launch of Make it in the Emirates. Our team developed the content strategy and creative direction for this campaign, while producing all of its launch videos.

Make it in the Emirates is the national campaign and call to action that supports Operation 300bn, a national industrial growth strategy. Launched by the Ministry of Industry and Advanced Technology, Operation 300bn aims to position the UAE as a global industrial hub by 2031. Overall, it will support 13,500 small and medium enterprises over the next 10 years. The strategy aims to: Enhance the attractiveness of the industrial sector for investors, support local products and suppliers, and encourage the adoption of advanced technology to grow and expand industry.

Culture Summit 2021

As the strategic digital and social media agency for Abu Dhabi Culture, Alsayegh was responsible for the conceptualization, curation, and event management of the Abu Dhabi Culture Summit 2021 and its digital communications. The Culture Summit, under the theme “The Cultural Economy and the Economy of Culture”, returned to Abu Dhabi for the fourth year in its first virtual edition. The three-day program saw the participation of over 100 global experts. It called for increased international collaboration, sustainable investment in the culture sector, and a new value proposition for culture on the public agenda.

2071

The Dubai Health Authority (DHA) held an innovation lab titled “Health Future Vision 2071” to envision the future of health in Dubai. Sets Two Guinness World Records

We have been the agency of choice for managing communications for Sheikh Zayed Festival, a unique global event. The event was recently ranked among the top 5 most important cultural events in the world according to a study done by Abu Dhabi Tourism.
